Student Activities

The animated scatter plot above displays the total number of activities in the course in relation to the duration of the course (2013J) Each circle displays a different course offering in that session

  • Green colored circle represents the course AAA (Social Science Course)
  • Blue Colored circle represents the course BBB (Social Science Course)
  • Red colored circle represents the course DDD (STEM course)

The size of the circles corresponds to the number of students currently enrolled in the course. The size of the circle will decrease as students deregister from the course. Increment on the timeline also represents the duration of the course relative to the start date.
